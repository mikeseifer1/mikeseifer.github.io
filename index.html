Michael Seifer

Michael Seifer, Vice President, is a member of the Core PM SMA Team in Princeton for BlackRock's Managed Account business within Multi-Asset Strategies (MAS). In his role, he partners with financial professionals and supports the firm's Managed Account business across multiple platforms and channels. His responsibilities include implementation and customization of Fixed Income solutions for client portfolios. Michael joined BlackRock following the merger with Merrill Lynch Investment Management (MLIM) in 2006. At MLIM, he was Assistant Vice President and Portfolio Manager in Private Investors, concentrating on municipal bonds. Before joining MLIM in 2006, he was an Assistant Municipal Bond Portfolio Manager at Lebenthal Asset Management. Mr. Seifer was also the Compliance Administrator for the Fixed Income division of Capital Markets at Advest Inc. From 1995 to 1998, he was a corporate bond trader for Advest's retail trading desk. Michael earned a BS degree in economics from the University of Delaware in 1994 and an MBA degree from New York University in 2001. Michael has been a CFA? Charterholder since 2002.
